Artist Statement

Climate change is a social and economic issue that I have acknowledged for
years. Living sustainably and trying to spread the word has always been a
practice for me, but many people do not do the same. Through my game, I
want to give a perspective on the end effects of climate change: sea
levels rising to the point that they're deadly. Today on Earth, we face
a future that is just as threatening due to the emissions of greenhouse
gases solely due to human activity.
